NOTICE TO CUSTOMERS

I hope this message finds you all safe and well

This is a quick note to say that I am currently implementing the changes necessary to allow me to safely re-open my barber shop which must comply with government guidelines. As soon as I am advised on when I can re-open I will notify you. Check my Google business page for subsequent updates. I might have to open by appointment only, depending on Government guidelines. If so there will be an online appointment system or you can phone to make an appointment.

The necessary changes I am making to the barber shop are as follows:

  • ALL HAIR MUST BE WASHED BEFORE VISITING FOR A HAIRCUT to protect the health of customers and staff.
  • There will be no cash till ….. CARD PAYMENTS ONLY PLEASE in line with government guidelines.
  • I am re-designing the shop to allow for SOCIAL DISTANCING ( 2 METRES) while hopefully keeping 2 chairs working at once to minimise waiting times. 1 chair with me and 1 chair with Aidan (he has completed his course and is ready and keen to assist you all with your mullets and any other interesting looks that you’ve been rocking!
  • When the shop is fully open you will be asked to queue outside so let’s hope the weather is kind!!!

Ok well I’ll get on with sorting the shop and you lot can carry on growing those mullets!!! Until my next update …….Stay Safe……… Love to you all!!!
